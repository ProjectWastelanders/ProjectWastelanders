The Game

Guardians of the Galaxy: The Bet is a Top-Down Action Shooting game set in the Marvel Universe. Play through completely different worlds with the Guardians of the Galaxy squad and battle against those who threaten the universe with unique weapons. Unlock different types of skills while progressing in the story and create all kinds of weapons to fight the evil.
